Danna's Story

You are about to read a passage soley about the life and interest's of Danna Moore. Danna was born and spent most of her childhood growing up in Houston, Texas. At the age of fifteen, Danna's family moved to St. Croix where she continued her education and later graduating from the Academy of the West Indies. After graduation, Danna left to Colorado to attend Colorado's Northwestern Community College. At age 18 she aspired be a truck driver so she signed up for yet another the community college located in Houston, Texas. After some time on the road Danna got married to her first husband, who also a truck driver at the same company. they then moved to his home town in Maine. After a while I moved baack to Aiken, SC. I went to Aiken Technical College to learn drafting. I met my second husband at Aiken Tech and we were married for 25 years and had two girls, Nicole who is not 25, and Karla who is 20. We divorced about 2 years ago. My last position was a mechanical design position at the Savannah River Site.

Danna enjoys Camping and hates exercise as she described it as "torture"
